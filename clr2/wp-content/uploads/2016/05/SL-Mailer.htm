Securities Market in India is majorly governed by the Securities Exchange Board of India (SEBI). A host of SEBI regulations govern the securities transaction in India like the Listing Regulations, ICDR Regulations, Prohibition of Insider Trading Regulations, Takeover Regulations etc. In the recent past many of these laws have seen significant amendments in them to promote larger transparency, ease of business and higher level of corporate governance for investor protection. These changes in a host of laws has an outreaching impact on the Corporates and Investors either directly or indirectly and business at large.

CorpKonnect’s full day Security Laws – Recent Developments Masterclass will enable corporate executives and practicing professionals to update their knowledge and provide relevant insights on the various amendments in Security Laws governing security transactions.
