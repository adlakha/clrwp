The GST is a reality now and the industry is almost sure of it's arrival either in April or some month later, but definitely in 2017. The Rajya Sabha has already passed the 122nd Constitutional Amendments Bill. 10 state assemblies till date have already ratified the Bill. But are we really ready for the migration to GST. It's implementation in India will require a great level of coordination from all corners of business. It will also require a lot of change in the supply chain management, costing analysis, invoicing methodology as well as your accounting software & documentation. 

 

To address this larger need of the hour, CorpKonnect is organizing a full day GST Implementation Masterclass which would throw valuable insights on what changes you need to do in your existing system and processes to comply with GST and what would be it’s impact on your business. Also we shall do in in-depth analysis of the Model GST Law to understand its intricacies.
